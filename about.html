About Me

My name is Michael Nayshaun Mathews. This weppage is dedicated to my father. I and my sisters never got much time to spend with him as a child. We were always waiting for him at home, and having brief visits while we could. When he did come in 2011 we were so excited, I got to do so much with him. But an accident around 2018 had made him ill, sending him into recovery. More years we spent visiting and waiting again. In 2022 he was home again, and we were set on taking care of him. But a mere month later, God had other plans. This page is a collection of the memories I have left, and to never forget what I once had.
